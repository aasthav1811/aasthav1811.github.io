[ 02 ] CAPABILITIES

What product teams actually get

01
BERT sentiment
RoBERTa fine-tuned on 124M tweets. Document + aspect-based, with confidence and a VADER fallback for offline.
02
Topic discovery
NMF clustering finds 8 auto-labeled topics with keyword weights and a sentiment split per cluster.
03
Trend forecasting
90-day history with a 14-day exponential-smoothing forecast and statistical anomaly detection.
04
Crisis detection
Scores legal, breach, outrage and viral signals into low/medium/high/critical — amplified by engagement.
05
Competitor intel
Extracts competitor mentions, compares sentiment and surfaces switch signals and opportunity gaps.
06
Live analyzer
Any text in → sentiment, confidence, crisis score and aspect breakdown out, in one call.
[ 03 ] PERFORMANCE

Measured, not marketed

Honest note on methodology

These come from the repo's own test harness — throughput and latency under batch and concurrent load. The model is cardiffnlp/twitter-roberta-base-sentiment-latest — trained on 124M tweets — with a VADER fallback. The platform's contribution is the pipeline around it.

MEASUREMENTVALUE
Batch throughput (100 posts)808 posts/sec
Avg request latency (sequential)~14 ms
Per-post analysis (transformer)~50 ms
Full dashboard payload~1 s
