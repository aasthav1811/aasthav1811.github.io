01 · Project Overview

From signal noise to emotional clarity

Support and engagement tools are blind to how users actually feel. This system changes that.

The Problem
Single-modal systems miss 60% of emotional signals
Facial analysis alone is fooled by poker faces. Text sentiment alone misses visual distress. Neither is sufficient — the gap costs businesses in churn, missed escalations, and poor care moments.
The Solution
Late-fusion multimodal architecture with explainability
Two encoder branches (ViT for vision, RoBERTa for text) feed a cross-modal attention fusion layer. The unified embedding powers emotion classification, risk scoring, and recommendations.
Technical Approach
Pre-trained encoders + learned fusion head — integration is the moat
FER2013-fine-tuned ViT for facial features + RoBERTa sentiment backbone. Fused via lightweight cross-modal attention. Disciplined integration at production quality.
Impact Metrics
Measurable performance lift across modalities
Each modality alone underperforms. The fusion delivers the real gain — a consistent 3–4% lift over the best single modality, with significantly better risk flag precision.

Fairness & explainability built in

Emotion AI without bias auditing is a liability. This is a feature, not an afterthought.

SHAP Explainability
Per-prediction attribution maps showing which facial regions and text keywords most influenced the output. Engineers and auditors can interrogate any single decision.
🛡️
Fairness Constraints
Equalized odds enforcement during fine-tuning to prevent systematic misclassification of any demographic group. Tracked as a first-class training metric.
📊
GradCAM Attribution
Gradient-weighted class activation maps overlay on the input face, showing exactly which pixel regions drove the emotion prediction — not a black box.
