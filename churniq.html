The Problem

Companies bleed revenue every month — silently

B2B SaaS companies lose 5–7% of their customer base monthly. Without intelligence, retention teams are reactive, underpowered, and too late.

🔍

No visibility into who will leave

Most companies only find out a customer is churning when they cancel — with no warning and no time to intervene.

❓

No explanation for why

Even when churn is tracked, teams have no per-customer signal. Every at-risk customer looks the same.

📧

No personalized action

Generic "we miss you" emails don't work. Customers feel unseen. Discounts get wasted on users who weren't leaving anyway.

Core Capabilities

Three systems. One outcome.

Predict who leaves before they do

An XGBoost classifier trained on 21 customer features produces a calibrated churn probability for every active account. SMOTE resolves the class imbalance problem common in churn datasets.

  • 21 behavioral + billing features engineered from raw data
  • SMOTE oversampling for the 27% minority churn class
  • Risk banding: low / medium / high with configurable thresholds
  • 5-segment behavioral clustering on top of risk scores
  • Quarterly retrain pipeline with drift monitoring

Know exactly why each customer is at risk

SHAP TreeExplainer generates per-customer feature attributions. Every prediction comes with a ranked list of factors pushing toward or away from churn — readable by both engineers and CSMs.

  • Per-customer local explanations, not just global importance
  • Red bars increase risk, green bars protect against it
  • Feeds directly into LLM prompt for grounded email drafts
  • Exposed in both dashboard UI and API response

Personalized retention — at scale

An AI agent reads the SHAP drivers, selects the most relevant intervention, and drafts a 120-word retention email that feels hand-written. Not a template. Not generic. Grounded in that customer's actual data.

  • T-learner causal uplift filters to true intervention candidates
  • Rule-based intervention selector (discount tier, CSM call, onboarding)
  • Groq (Llama 3.1-70B) drafts grounded in SHAP drivers
  • Full output via FastAPI — plugs into HubSpot, Salesforce, any CRM
