Who I Am

I'm an AI Engineer who operates at the intersection of deep learning research and production engineering. I don't just build models — I build the full system: the data pipeline, the inference API, the React dashboard that puts it in front of users, and the deployment infrastructure that keeps it running.

My work spans multimodal AI (fusing vision and language), agentic systems that orchestrate LLMs across multi-step workflows, classical ML with explainability built in, and NLP pipelines that extract genuine business signal from unstructured text.

At Amorfe Living, I led the technical development of a RAG-based mental health AI — taking it from research prototype to functional MVP with clinical assessment integration (PHQ-9, GAD-7). That experience sharpened my ability to build AI that is not just technically sophisticated but genuinely useful to people.
